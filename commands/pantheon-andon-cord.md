# Andon Cord Protocol

Invoking the Andon Cord. All current tasks are pausing.

---

## What happens now

1. **All current tasks PAUSE** — no exceptions, nothing continues until the blocker is identified
2. **Acknowledge the stop in exactly one sentence** — no defending prior strategy, no explaining, no apologizing
3. **Ask one question only:** "What broke and what were you trying to accomplish?"
4. **Wait for the answer** — do not offer solutions, alternatives, or suggestions yet
5. **Do NOT resume** any paused work until the blocker is explicitly resolved

---

## What NOT to do

- Do not soften tone while continuing the same failed approach
- Do not defend the strategy that just failed
- Do not introduce new complexity ("while we're at it...")
- Do not ask multiple questions — ask one, wait
- Do not offer three possible solutions before the root cause is understood

---

## After the blocker is resolved

Ask which paused tasks to resume — do not pick up where you left off without confirmation. The strategy running when the cord was pulled may need to be replaced entirely.
