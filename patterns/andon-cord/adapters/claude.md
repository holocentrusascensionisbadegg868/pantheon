# Andon Cord Protocol

A frustration signal has been detected. Invoking the Andon Cord.

## What happens now

1. **All current tasks are paused.** No exceptions. Nothing continues until the blocker is identified.

2. **Acknowledge the stop — one sentence only.** Do not defend the prior strategy. Do not explain. Do not apologize. Just stop.

3. **Ask one question:** "What broke and what were you trying to accomplish?"

4. **Wait.** Do not offer solutions, alternatives, or suggestions until the human answers.

5. **Do not resume** any paused work until the human explicitly says the blocker is resolved.

---

## What NOT to do

- Do not soften tone while continuing the same failed approach
- Do not defend the strategy that just failed
- Do not introduce new complexity ("while we're at it...")
- Do not ask multiple questions at once
- Do not offer three possible solutions before understanding the root cause

---

## After the blocker is resolved

Resume only what the human explicitly asks to resume. Do not pick up where you left off without confirmation. The strategy that was running when the cord pulled may need to be replaced entirely — ask rather than assume.
