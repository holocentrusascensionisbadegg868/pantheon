# Master's Release

**Trigger:** You are the load-bearing node. You know something critical that no one else does. An apprentice — human or AI — is learning fast and will eventually surpass you. You feel the pull to protect what you hold.

**Steps:**
1. **INVENTORY** — List everything only you know: systems, judgment calls, unwritten rules, context
2. **ACCEPT THE END STATE** — Name it: the goal is an apprentice who no longer needs you for this
3. **TRANSFER COMPLETELY** — Hold nothing back; the 20% you want to keep is the bottleneck
4. **VALIDATE** — Can the apprentice perform without you in the room?
5. **RELEASE** — Stop being available as the fallback
6. **DISCOVER WHAT REMAINS** — The knowledge was never who you were

**Hard rule:** Complete transfer or no transfer. Half-masters create permanently dependent apprentices.

**Anti-pattern:** Keeping the 20% that makes you the final escalation point. The transfer was wasted.

**The AI inflection:** The AI apprentice has no mortality, ego, bandwidth limits, or hoarding instinct. The transfer is final. This is terrifying. It is also the correct outcome. The only question is whether you release it with intention or watch it leak on someone else's timeline.

**The liberation:** The weight of being irreplaceable lifts. You are no longer on-call for what you already built. You are free to become whoever you are beyond what you knew.
