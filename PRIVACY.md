# Privacy Policy — Pantheon

Effective date: March 24, 2026

---

## What we collect

Pantheon does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

- **API keys** — entered in the Gem Runner are stored in your browser's localStorage only. They never leave your device.
- **Inputs** — text or files you submit to the Gem Runner are sent directly from your browser to your chosen API provider (OpenRouter, etc.). Pantheon has no server that receives or logs this data.
- **Usage** — no analytics, no tracking, no cookies.

## Automated content

The Pantheon research pipeline posts AI-generated content to X (Twitter) and Threads on a scheduled basis. No user data is involved in this process.

## Third-party services

When you use the Gem Runner, your input is sent to the API provider you configured (e.g. OpenRouter). Their privacy policies apply to that data.

## Changes

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above will be updated. The current version is always available at this URL.
